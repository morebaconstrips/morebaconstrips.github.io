Adversarial Examples Evading deepfake-image detectors with white-box attacks

Jan 2023 - Aug 2023

The project involved conducting in-depth research into potential white and black box attacks on a ResNet50, aiming to significantly reduce the classifier’s accuracy to near 0%. Subsequently, white-box attacks were implemented on a ResNet50 model fine-tuned with the TrueFace dataset provided by the University of Trento. The results were remarkable, as we successfully lowered the Neural Network’s accuracy from 100% to nearly 0%.
