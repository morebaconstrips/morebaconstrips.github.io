Enhancing Thera mental health app: integrating AI-powered safety measures for improved user well-being

Jul 2023 - Aug 2023

Our team played a pivotal role in advancing Thera (a Lithuanian mental health app) by integrating AI-driven safety measures. By implementing a carefully designed decision-tree logic and dynamic AI filtering, we ensured that user interactions with the app were guided towards safer outcomes. Our contribution significantly bolstered Thera’s commitment to user well-being, offering enhanced support and tailored responses while mitigating potential risks, thereby fostering a safer and more effective mental health journey for every user.Our
